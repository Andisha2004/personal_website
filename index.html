Camp Mosaic Counselor (August 2023)

My experience as a camp counselor at Camp Mosaic in Edison, NJ, during August 2023 was a profoundly rewarding opportunity. In this role, I volunteered my time and energy to guide and support children aged 6-13 throughout their camp experience. One of the most impactful aspects of this role was my involvement in creating a more inclusive and respectful environment within the camp. Together with my fellow counselors, we devised and implemented an innovative approach known as the "strikes" method. This method served as a proactive strategy to address instances of bullying, fostering a culture of empathy, cooperation, and mutual respect among the campers. By employing this method, we aimed to create a safe and harmonious atmosphere where every child felt valued and included. As a camp counselor, I had the privilege of not only organizing engaging activities but also serving as a positive role model and mentor to the campers. This experience not only allowed me to develop my leadership skills but also deepened my understanding of the importance of fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment for young individuals. My time as a camp counselor at Camp Mosaic underscored the significance of effective communication, teamwork, and empathy in building a supportive community. It was a heartwarming experience to witness the positive impact our efforts had on the campers, as they blossomed into more confident, respectful, and harmonious individuals. This experience reinforced my commitment to making a positive difference in the lives of others, a value that I carry with me in both my personal and professional endeavors.
